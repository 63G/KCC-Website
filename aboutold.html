About Us

We are a vibrant community at KFUPM dedicated to fostering excellence in consulting. Our club offers members opportunities to develop their skills, network with professionals, and gain insights into the consulting industry.

Our Mission, Vision, and Values

Mission: To empower students by providing them with the skills, knowledge, and network needed to succeed in the consulting industry.

Vision: To be the leading student consulting club recognized for shaping the future leaders of the consulting world.

Values: Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration, and Leadership.

Meet Our Team

Our team consists of passionate students and faculty advisors dedicated to the success of our members and the club.

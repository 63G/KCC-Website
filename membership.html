Membership Requirements

Joining the KFUPM Consulting Club is open to all who are passionate about consulting and professional development. However, we have a few requirements to ensure that our community remains as impactful and dedicated as possible.

  • Currently enrolled at KFUPM or an alumni
  • Commitment to participate in club events and activities
  • Interest in pursuing a career in consulting or related fields
  • Submission of a completed application form
  • Agreement to abide by the club's code of conduct and ethics

If you meet these requirements and are excited about contributing to and benefiting from the club, we encourage you to apply.
